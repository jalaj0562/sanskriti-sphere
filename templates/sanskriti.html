Powered by GPT and IndicNLP. Speaks 22 Indian languages plus 15 global ones. Adjusts vocabulary for children vs researchers. References your declared interests — architecture, art, warfare, religion. Answers follow-up questions in real-time. Historical accuracy verified with Ministry of Tourism databases and peer-reviewed scholarship.

Historical Re-enactments
Witness key historical moments with holographic visuals — royal ceremonies, Mughal court, ancient battles, and sacred festivals.
Tap to expand ▾
Motion-captured animations combined with AI crowd simulations recreate royal durbars, religious processions, construction scenes, and pivotal battles. Each scene is scripted from primary historical sources and reviewed by historians at IIT and ASI. Users can choose their perspective: audience, participant, or aerial observer. Each monument has 3–5 selectable re-enactment events.
📍
Interactive Hotspots
Touch any glowing pin on the canvas for instant AI-powered insights — dating, artist identity, construction techniques, and cultural significance.
Tap to expand ▾
Over 50,000 hotspots mapped across 500+ sites. Each links to a knowledge graph containing provenance data, related artworks, comparative architecture from other cultures, and bibliographic references. YOLO-based image recognition identifies unlabelled artifacts from user photographs with 94% accuracy. Hotspot pins animate and pulse to guide user attention to key points of interest.
🌍
Remote Site Access
Explore physically inaccessible heritage sites — restricted cave interiors, fragile ruins, remote mountain temples.
Tap to expand ▾
Many of India's most fragile sites are closed to physical visitors. Sanskriti Sphere provides the only immersive access to sites like the Bagh Caves, Undavalli cave shrines, and ASI-restricted excavations. Drone photogrammetry captures these sites without physical intrusion. Virtual visits generate no carbon emissions and cause zero wear to irreplaceable surfaces.
🌐
Multilingual Support
Full platform support for 22 Indian languages and 15 global languages with authentic regional audio guides and cultural phrasebooks.
Tap to expand ▾
Built on IndicNLP and Bhashini APIs. Every hotspot, story, and quiz available in Devanagari, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Odia, Punjabi, Gujarati, Marathi, and more. Text-to-speech uses authentic regional voice actors — not synthetic TTS. Cultural phrasebooks teach regional idioms. Audio guides can be downloaded for offline use in remote areas.

About the Platform
AI-Integrated AR/VR Heritage Platform
Sanskriti Sphere is India's most ambitious digital heritage initiative — a bridge between 5,000 years of tradition and 21st-century technology.

🎯 Our Mission

To make India's extraordinary cultural heritage accessible to every person on Earth — breaking barriers of geography, language, and physical ability through immersive technology aligned with the Incredible India initiative and Digital India programme.
