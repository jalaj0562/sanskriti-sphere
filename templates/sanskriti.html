Platform Capabilities
Every Grain of History, Preserved
Click any card to expand full details. Sanskriti Sphere uses AI, AR and VR to bring heritage alive.
🥽
Immersive 3D Environments
Photo-realistic reconstructions built using photogrammetry and Unity Engine. Walk every corridor as it appeared centuries ago.
Our 3D environments are built from thousands of photographs processed through photogrammetry. Each monument is scanned at millimetre precision. Textures include real material data — marble roughness, stone weathering, painted plaster. Environments update seasonally to reflect how the site looks at different times of year and day.
🤖
AI Storytelling Guide
A personal AI historian narrates your journey in 22 languages, adapting to your interests using GPT-powered NLP.
Powered by GPT and IndicNLP, the guide speaks 22 Indian languages plus 15 global ones. It adjusts vocabulary for children vs researchers, references your declared interests (architecture, art, warfare, religion), and answers questions in real-time. Accuracy is verified with Ministry of Tourism databases and peer-reviewed scholarship.
📜
Historical Re-enactments
Witness key historical moments with holographic visuals — royal ceremonies, ancient battles, festivals.
Motion-captured animations combined with AI crowd simulations recreate royal durbars, religious processions, construction scenes, and pivotal battles. Every scene is scripted from primary historical sources and reviewed by historians at IIT and ASI. Users can choose their perspective — audience, participant, or aerial observer.
📍
Interactive Hotspots
Touch any artifact or architectural element on the canvas for instant AI-powered insights — dating, artist, cultural significance.
Over 50,000 hotspots mapped across 500+ sites. Each links to a knowledge graph containing provenance data, related artworks, comparative architecture, and bibliographic references. YOLO-based image recognition identifies unlabelled artifacts from user photographs with 94% accuracy.
🌍
Remote Site Access
Explore physically inaccessible heritage sites — deep caves, fragile ruins, restricted monuments — safely.
Many of India's most fragile sites are closed to physical visitors. Sanskriti Sphere provides the only immersive access to sites like the Bagh Caves, Undavalli cave shrines, and several ASI-restricted excavations. Drone photogrammetry captures these sites without physical intrusion or risk to the monuments.
🌐
Multilingual Support
Full support for 22 Indian languages and 15 global languages with authentic regional audio guides.
Built on IndicNLP and Bhashini APIs. Every page, hotspot, story, and quiz is available in Devanagari, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Odia, Punjabi, Gujarati, Marathi, and more. Text-to-speech uses authentic regional voice actors — not synthetic TTS — for cultural authenticity and natural delivery.

About the Platform
AI-Integrated AR/VR Heritage Platform
Sanskriti Sphere is India's most ambitious digital heritage project — a bridge between tradition and modernity.

🎯 Our Mission

To make India's cultural heritage accessible to every person on Earth — breaking barriers of geography, language, and physical ability through immersive technology aligned with the Incredible India initiative.
